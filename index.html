About

Designed to be easily accessible, this is a collection of improvisational games that I have found useful and entertaining. To read about my experiences learning and teaching through improvisation, join a mailing list to be notified when my new book, Long Form Learning: Games, Bridges, Incans, and Vampires is available, or watch excerpts from the text as they develop online.

These games aren't mine. I wrote the descriptions of them you find below, but almost all of them will have come from another source originally, either a book or a person. My role here is a collector, and someone who assembles known elements into a new pattern.
